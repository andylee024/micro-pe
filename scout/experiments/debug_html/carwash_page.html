Business Description

Excellent Passive Income Stream.

Established Self-Serve Car Wash - Coalinga's Only Full-Service Option

This profitable car wash operation has served Coalinga, CA for over 50 years, establishing itself as the sole self-serve car wash facility in a town of 18,000 residents. Located on a prime corner lot in the heart of town, this business generates strong passive income with minimal day-to-day management requirements.

Financial Performance:
- Annual Revenue: $90,000 ($20,000 credit card, $70,000 cash)
- Cash Flow (SDE): $72,700
- Monthly Operating Expenses: Approximately $1,200 (utilities, taxes, insurance)
- Established 1969 with consistent revenue history

Property & Assets:
- 435 sq ft building on large corner lot
- Real estate included ($300,000 value)
- Additional 5,700 sq ft fenced vacant lot for expansion
- FF&E valued at $625,000 with recent updates
- $6,000 inventory of parts, hoses, soap, tools, and air compressors included

Competitive Advantages:
- Monopoly position as only self-serve option in market
- Limited competition (one higher-priced tunnel wash at gas station)
- Prime downtown location with high visibility
- Can be operated remotely with proper site management

Growth Opportunities:
The substantial vacant lot offers multiple expansion possibilities including additional wash bays, Tesla charging stations, oil change services, RV storage, food truck rentals, or rental buildings for additional income streams.

Owner Support:
Seller will provide comprehensive training and education for smooth transition. Seller financing available with substantial down payment.

This turnkey operation offers an excellent opportunity for passive income in a stable market with significant expansion potential.
